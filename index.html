About GutSimple

Medicine Should Never Feel Like a Wall

Too many patients leave appointments more confused than when they arrived. Medical information online is either oversimplified or inaccessibly technical. GutSimple was built to close that gap.

We believe that an informed patient is a healthier patient. When you understand what's happening in your body, you ask better questions, follow through on treatment, and feel less afraid.

Our content is written by gastroenterology professionals and reviewed for accuracy — then rewritten by humans who know that "gastroparesis" needs more than a dictionary definition.
